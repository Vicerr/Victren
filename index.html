About Me

Hi, my name is VICTOR ODOI and I am a FRONTEND WEB DEVELOPER and a COMPUTER TECHNICIAN with a passion for technology and love for creating responsive and functional websites.

As a front-end web developer, I have over 2 years of experience with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, taking designs from web designers and UI/UX designers to build responsive, accessable and user-friendly websites that are optimised for both desktop and mobile devices. I also build web components to share what I have learnt to other developeres to expand my knowledge

As a computer technician, I have a deep understanding of computer hardware and software, and am skilled in troubleshooting and repairing technical issues. I enjoy solving complex problems and working with users to help them better understand their technology.

My skills and experiences have been honed through years of working in the industry, and I am constantly seeking to expand my knowledge and stay up to date with the latest trends and technologies.

When I am not working on computers, I enjoy spending time reading or conducting online research about my environment.

I look forward to continue my work as a front-end developer and computer technician, and look forward to collaborating with others to create innovative and effective solutions for a variety of technical challenges.
